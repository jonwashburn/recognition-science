anchor scale $\mu^\star = 182.2~\mathrm{GeV}$ via the $\varphi$-ladder.
\end{abstract}

\begin{theorem}[Weinberg Angle at Tree Level]\label{thm:weinberg}
In the RS framework, the tree-level Weinberg angle is determined exactly:
\begin{equation}

\begin{equation}

\end{proof}
